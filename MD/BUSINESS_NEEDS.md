## 01 — Dedicated Content Person

**Category:** Marketing
**Status:** Open
**Priority:** Critical
**Blocking:** The entire SEO/AEO strategy. Without consistent publishing, the discoverability strategy doesn't execute.

### What's Needed
A person whose primary job is producing content for foamauto.com — blog posts, FAQ pages, city hub pages, operator guides, and customer education content. This is not a part-time add-on to someone else's role.

### Volume Required
- 2 blog posts per week at launch (minimum)
- 4 blog posts per week by Month 9
- City hub pages and neighborhood pages as markets expand
- Ongoing FAQ updates as product evolves

### What This Person Needs to Be Good At
- Writing clearly for two audiences simultaneously (operators and customers)
- Basic SEO understanding — keyword placement, internal linking, heading structure
- Ability to write in FOAM's brand voice (direct, human, not corporate)
- Some understanding of the auto detailing industry (or willingness to learn fast)

### Options to Explore
- Full-time content hire (ideal for Year 1 growth)
- Fractional content lead + freelance writers (lower cost, more management overhead)
- AI-assisted workflow with a part-time editor (minimum viable, higher risk on quality)
- Industry-connected freelancer from the detailing community (authentic voice advantage)

### Decision Criteria
Whoever fills this role needs to produce content that ranks and converts — not content that just exists. Evaluate candidates on writing samples that demonstrate both clarity and SEO awareness.

## 02 — SEO / Keyword Tracking Tool

**Category:** Marketing / Technical
**Status:** Open
**Priority:** High
**Blocking:** Ability to measure ranking progress, validate keyword strategy, identify content gaps

### What's Needed
A tool that tracks foamauto.com keyword rankings over time, shows competitor rankings for target terms, identifies backlink profile, and surfaces new keyword opportunities.

### Options
- **Ahrefs** — industry standard, best backlink data, ~$99-$199/month
- **Semrush** — strong all-around, slightly more expensive, good for PPC data too
- **Moz** — lighter, cheaper (~$49/month), less data depth
- **Google Search Console** — free, essential regardless of which tool is chosen, but limited on its own

### Recommendation
Start with Google Search Console (free, mandatory) plus Ahrefs Starter or equivalent at minimum. Do not run an SEO strategy blind. Knowing which keywords are moving and which content is ranking is how the content strategy gets smarter over time.

### Decision Criteria
Tool must track: keyword position over time, competitor rankings for target terms, backlink acquisition, and content performance by page.

## 03 — App Store Optimization (ASO) Review

**Category:** Marketing
**Status:** Open
**Priority:** High
**Blocking:** App discoverability in App Store and Google Play; ASO is AEO for mobile

### What's Needed
A professional review and optimization of the FOAM App Store listing (iOS) and Google Play listing (Android) before public launch. This includes title, subtitle, keyword field, description, and screenshot strategy.

### Why This Can't Be an Afterthought
App store search drives a significant portion of installs for marketplace apps. The listing is also what Siri, Google Assistant, and AI systems pull from when answering "what's the best app for [category]." A weak listing loses installs that should be free.

### Options
- DIY using ASO tool guidance (AppFollow, AppTweak, MobileAction)
- ASO consultant for a one-time audit and optimization (~$500-$2,000)
- Ongoing ASO management (more relevant post-launch with live data)

## 05 — Schema Markup Implementation

**Category:** Technical
**Status:** Open
**Priority:** Critical
**Blocking:** Both SEO ranking signals and AEO citation eligibility

### What's Needed
Proper structured data markup implemented across all major page types on foamauto.com before launch. This is a technical implementation task, not a content task.

### Required Schema Types
- `SoftwareApplication` — on /for-detailers and /for-shops pages
- `LocalBusiness` — on every operator profile page
- `FAQPage` — on every FAQ section across the site
- `BreadcrumbList` — on all city and neighborhood hub pages
- `AggregateRating` — on city hub pages once reviews are live

### Who Handles This
The developer building foamauto.com. This needs to be scoped into the marketing site build, not added as an afterthought post-launch. Validate implementation using Google's Rich Results Test before going live.

## 14 — Analytics Infrastructure

**Category:** Technical
**Status:** Open
**Priority:** High (needed at launch)
**Blocking:** Understanding what's working; required for investor reporting

### What's Needed
Analytics tracking across: foamauto.com (web), the iOS app, and the Android app. Minimum viable tracking covers: sessions, signups, bookings completed, operator activation rate, and customer retention.

### Options
- **PostHog** — product analytics, open source option, excellent for startup stage
- **Mixpanel** — industry standard for mobile apps, strong funnel analysis
- **Amplitude** — strong retention and behavioral analytics
- **Google Analytics 4** — free, good for web, weaker for app

### Recommendation
PostHog or Mixpanel for product analytics. Google Analytics 4 for web alongside it (it's free and has SEO-adjacent value in Search Console integration). Set this up before soft launch — retroactive analytics don't exist.
