# Vision

## The One-Liner
The operating system for mobile auto detailing — where customers find and book the best detailers, and detailers run their entire business.

## The Problem
Mobile auto detailing is a fragmented, word-of-mouth industry running on Instagram DMs, Venmo requests, and Google Calendar hacks. Customers can't easily find vetted, reliable detailers. Detailers — whether solo or running a crew — have no purpose-built tool to manage their business. Everyone is duct-taping together five different apps to do what one should do.

## The Opportunity
The Cut solved this exact problem for barbers. Before it existed, barbers managed bookings through phone calls and social media. The Cut didn't beat a dominant competitor — it beat chaos. Mobile detailing is in the same place right now. No dominant platform exists. No purpose-built operator OS exists. The market is ready.

## The Solution
A single app with three distinct experiences:
- **Customers** discover, book, and build relationships with their detailer
- **Detailers** manage their entire business — bookings, payments, customers, crew, and growth
- **Crew members** see their jobs, navigate to locations, and track their earnings

## Why This Wins
Most booking apps treat the service provider as a lead generation afterthought. We treat detailers as the product. If detailers can't leave — because their entire business lives here — customers have no choice but to follow. Lock in supply, demand follows.

## The Founder's Edge
This wasn't built by someone who studied the detailing industry. It was built by someone who ran a mobile detailing operation called Foam Auto Spa — who knows what it feels like to chase down payments, lose track of customer vehicle notes, and manage crew schedules through text messages. That operator experience is the product's unfair advantage.

## Long-Term Vision
Become the default infrastructure layer for the mobile detailing industry — the platform every detailer builds their business on, every customer trusts, and every investor in the space has to reckon with.

- 4.7+ average detailer rating
- $50K+ in GMV processed
- Atlanta market saturation before expansion
