# Vision

## The One-Liner
The operating system for auto detailing — where customers find and book the best mobile detailers and local shops, and operators run their entire business from one place.

## The Problem
Auto detailing is a billion-dollar industry running on chaos. Mobile detailers manage their routes through Instagram DMs and Venmo. Shop owners track their bays on whiteboards and their appointments in a notes app one cracked screen away from disappearing. Whether you're sending your crew to a customer's driveway or running a four-bay operation on the east side of Atlanta, the infrastructure was never built for you. Everyone is duct-taping together five different apps to do what one should do — and the customers trying to find and trust a good detailer are stuck in the same chaos from the other side.

## The Opportunity
The Cut solved this exact problem for barbers. Before it existed, barbers managed bookings through phone calls and social media. The Cut didn't beat a dominant competitor — it beat chaos. Auto detailing is in the same place right now. No dominant platform exists for mobile operators. No purpose-built OS exists for the independent shop down the street. The market across both formats is ready and waiting.

## The Solution
A single platform with three distinct operator experiences and one customer-facing marketplace:
- **Customers** discover, book, and build relationships with mobile detailers and local shops in one unified feed — they choose whether someone comes to them or they drop their car off
- **Detailers and shop operators** manage their entire business — bookings, payments, customers, crew, bay management, and growth — from one place built for how detailing actually works
- **Crew members** see their assigned jobs, navigate to locations or bays, complete work, and track their earnings

## Why This Wins
Most booking apps treat the service provider as a lead generation afterthought. FOAM treats operators as the product — across both service models. A mobile detailer who has 90 days of customer history, route data, and crew records inside FOAM can't leave. A shop operator with 90 days of bay utilization, walk-in conversion, and service performance data inside FOAM can't leave either. Lock in supply on both sides of the detailing industry, and demand follows.

## The Founder's Edge
This wasn't built by someone who studied the detailing industry. It was built by someone who ran it — both sides of it. Foam Auto Spa was a mobile van AND three physical shops in Atlanta. The founder knows what it means to manage a crew across a route and a crew across bays at the same time. To lose a customer's vehicle notes on the mobile side and lose track of walk-in conversion on the shop side simultaneously. That dual operator experience — which still continues today through ongoing shop logistics — is the product's unfair advantage over every competitor who only understood one side of this industry, or neither.

## Long-Term Vision
Become the default infrastructure layer for the entire auto detailing industry — the platform every mobile detailer and every independent shop builds their business on, every customer trusts, and every investor in the space has to reckon with.
