# Roadmap

## Guiding Principle

Build what proves the loop first. Build what creates lock-in second. Build what drives growth third. Never build V2 features while V1 has unsolved problems.

Discoverability infrastructure (domain, schema markup, App Store listings, content, directory submissions) is part of the Foundation phase — not a post-launch task. The domain needs index history before customers arrive.

## Parking Lot (Validated, No Timeline Yet)

These ideas have clear strategic logic. Not prioritized because earlier layers need to prove out first. Each item has a prerequisite — do not pull from this list without validating it.

**FOAM Advance (Revenue-Based Financing)**
Short-term capital for operators underwritten on platform GMV. Flat fee model, repaid via booking deductions over 90–120 days. Prerequisite: 18+ months of operator GMV data and an active BaaS partner agreement. Full structure in MONETIZATION_STRATEGY.md.

**FOAM Business Debit**
Co-branded operator debit card. FOAM earns interchange. Expenses auto-categorize against the P&L dashboard. Prerequisite: BaaS partner live, 200+ active operators on platform. Full structure in MONETIZATION_STRATEGY.md.

**Sponsored Operator Placement**
Operators pay to rank higher in neighborhood and city search results. High-margin, low-infrastructure revenue. Prerequisite: Organic marketplace feels full — placement value only exists when there’s real competition for visibility.

**Brand Advertising Partnerships**
Sponsored content in operator newsletter, co-branded seasonal campaigns, featured placement in supply marketplace. Prerequisite: 500+ operators on the platform with an active weekly newsletter.

**Certification Program**
FOAM-branded operator credentials (FOAM Certified Detailer, Ceramic Specialist, Paint Correction Pro). $199–499 per certification, $79–99 annual renewal. Certified operators rank higher in search results. Prerequisite: One credible training partner signed. Targeted for Year 2 active build.

**White-Label for Franchise Operations**
Platform licensed to detailing franchise brands (Ziebart, DetailXPerts). They bring the operator network; FOAM provides infrastructure. Prerequisite: Platform stability across multiple markets proven. Year 2+.

**API Access for Enterprise or Fleet Integrations**
Prerequisite: Multiple enterprise accounts signed and actively using the platform.

**Operator-to-Operator Referral Program**
Operator brings in a peer, earns platform credit. Low-cost supply acquisition channel. Prerequisite: Active operator community of 100+.

**Desktop/Tablet Owner Dashboard**
Expanded view for Crew tier operators managing complex multi-tech operations.

**Dedicated Shop Operator Experience**
Bay view, walk-in queue management for fixed-location operators.

**Headlight Restoration and Paint Correction Specialty Categories**
Expanded service categories beyond standard detailing. Prerequisite: Core booking and marketplace working well in existing categories.

**foamauto.com/resources Knowledge Base**
Operator business education hub. SEO play and retention play simultaneously. Prerequisite: Consistent blog publishing proven and audience established.

**Data Monetization**
Aggregated, non-PII behavioral data sold to auto insurers, real estate developers, and private equity. Prerequisite: Legal structuring complete, significant market density across multiple cities. Year 3+.
